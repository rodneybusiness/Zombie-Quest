## Getting Started

### First Steps

1. The game begins on **Hennepin Avenue** outside First Avenue
2. You can choose your backstory (affects dialogue and endings):
   - **Purist**: Never sold out, kept the faith
   - **Sellout**: Made it big but abandoned the scene
   - **Survivor**: Outlasted everyone else
3. Your goal: Navigate the zombie-infested venues to reach Maya

## Game Mechanics

### The Verb System

Zombie Quest uses a classic point-and-click verb system:

- **WALK**: Move to a location (default)
- **LOOK**: Examine objects for information
- **USE**: Interact with objects or use items
- **TALK**: Speak with NPCs

**Tip**: Always LOOK at everything first to gather information!

### Health System

- You have **3 hearts** of health
- Zombies deal **1 damage** when they touch you
- After taking damage, you're **invincible for 1.5 seconds** (blue glow)
- Reaching 0 health = Game Over

### Musical Zombie Mechanics

Musical items create zones of effect that affect nearby zombies:

#### Zombie Types & Music Preferences

| Zombie Type | Loves | Likes | Dislikes |
|-------------|-------|-------|----------|
| **Scene Kid** | New Wave, Electronic | Ambient | Guitar |
| **Bouncer** | Punk, Guitar | - | New Wave, Electronic |
| **Rocker** | Guitar, Punk | - | Electronic |
| **DJ** | Electronic | New Wave, Ambient | Guitar |

#### Musical Item Effects

| Item | Music Type | Radius | Effect |
|------|------------|--------|--------|
| **Guitar Pick** | Guitar | 150px | Rockers dance, Scene kids slow |
| **Demo Tape** | New Wave | 120px | Scene kids entranced |
| **Setlist** | Ambient | 100px | All types calm slightly |
| **Vinyl Record** | Punk | 180px | Powerful effect on all types |

#### Zombie States

- **Hostile** (Red): Chases and damages you
- **Entranced** (Yellow): Frozen in place, harmless
- **Dancing** (Green): Swaying, harmless
- **Remembering** (Blue): Lucid, may help you

## Walkthrough

### Chapter 1: Hennepin Avenue

**Starting Location**: Outside First Avenue

#### Objectives
1. Get the Gig Flyer
2. Enter Let It Be Records
3. Trade flyer for Demo Tape

#### Step-by-Step

1. **LOOK at Marquee** - Learn about tonight's show
2. **Walk to Poster Kiosk** (left side)
3. **USE Poster Kiosk** - Obtain Gig Flyer
4. **Walk to Record Store Door** (center)
5. **USE Record Store Door** - Enter the store

**Zombies**: 2 zombies patrol the street. Use the walkable zones to avoid them.

---

### Chapter 2: Let It Be Records

**Location**: Inside the record store

#### Objectives
1. Trade Gig Flyer for Demo Tape
2. Navigate to KJRR radio station

#### Step-by-Step

1. **LOOK at everything** to get your bearings
2. **Walk to Record Clerk**
3. **TALK to Record Clerk** - Opens dialogue
4. **Select "Show Gig Flyer"** in dialogue (requires Gig Flyer)
5. Receive **Demo Tape**
6. **Exit through Street Door** or explore more

#### Optional Exploration
- **LOOK at Listening Booth** - Flavor text
- **LOOK at New Arrivals Bin** - Learn about local bands
- **LOOK at Rare Vinyl Section** - Locked, need to come back later

<details>
<summary><strong>Puzzle Solution: Getting the Demo Tape</strong></summary>

**Problem**: Clerk won't give you the demo tape without proof you're "scene."

**Solution**:
1. Get Gig Flyer from Poster Kiosk outside First Avenue
2. Show flyer to clerk
3. Clerk gives you Demo Tape

**Alternative**: None - this is the only path for this puzzle.
</details>

---

### Chapter 3: KJRR College Radio

**Location**: Basement radio station

#### Objectives
1. Trade Demo Tape for Backstage Pass
2. Return to First Avenue

#### Step-by-Step

1. **From Hennepin Avenue**, USE Alley to KJRR
2. **TALK to DJ Rotten**
3. **Select "Offer Demo Tape"** in dialogue
4. Receive **Backstage Pass**
5. **Exit through Alley Door**

#### Optional Exploration
- **LOOK at On-Air Light** - Flavor text
- **USE Request Line Phone** - Make a request
- **LOOK at Poster Wall** - Local band history

<details>
<summary><strong>Puzzle Solution: Getting Backstage Pass</strong></summary>

**Problem**: Need access to First Avenue backstage area.

**Solution**:
1. Get Demo Tape from Record Store Clerk
2. Bring Demo Tape to DJ Rotten at KJRR
3. Trade for Backstage Pass

**Why it works**: DJ Rotten needs fresh music for the airwaves. The demo tape is exactly what he wants.
</details>

---

### Chapter 4: Backstage at First Avenue

**Location**: Behind the stage

#### Objectives
1. Get past the zombie bouncer
2. Collect the Setlist
3. Access the Green Room

#### Step-by-Step

1. **From Hennepin Avenue**, USE First Avenue Door
2. **Select USE with Backstage Pass selected** - Bouncer lets you through
3. **USE Setlist Crate** - Obtain Setlist
4. **USE Flight Cases** - Obtain Guitar Pick
5. **USE Green Room Door** - Enter Green Room

#### Navigation Tips
- The backstage has **multiple zombies**
- Use musical items to pacify them if needed
- Monitor Mix and Makeup Mirrors are safe zones

---

### Chapter 5: Green Room

**Location**: Band's private room

#### Objectives
1. Collect Vinyl Record
2. Prepare for final confrontation

#### Step-by-Step

1. **LOOK at everything** for backstory
2. **USE Record Collection** - Obtain Vinyl Record
3. You now have all major musical items
4. **Optional**: Explore other areas

#### Items to Collect
- **Vinyl Record**: From Record Collection (most powerful musical item)
- **Guitar Pick**: From Flight Cases in backstage
- **Setlist**: From Setlist Crate in backstage
- **Demo Tape**: Already traded to DJ Rotten

## Item Combinations

### Musical Items Usage

Musical items don't combine but have different effects on zombies:

| Item | Best Used On | Effect |
|------|--------------|--------|
| **Guitar Pick** | Rockers, Bouncers | Makes them dance or remember |
| **Demo Tape** | Scene Kids, DJs | Entrances them |
| **Setlist** | All Types | Mild calming effect |
| **Vinyl Record** | All Types | Strongest effect overall |

### Strategic Item Use

**Scenario**: Surrounded by multiple zombie types

**Solution**:
1. Identify zombie types (Scene, Bouncer, Rocker, DJ)
2. Use Vinyl Record for area-wide effect
3. Or use type-specific items for targeted pacification

**Tip**: Musical items create temporary zones. Effects wear off after a few seconds!

## Music Mechanics

### How Music Affects Zombies

Music creates **invisible zones** around the player when a musical item is used:

1. **Radius**: Each item has a different effective radius
2. **Intensity**: Distance from source reduces intensity
3. **Affinity**: Each zombie type has preferences
4. **Duration**: Effects last 3-5 seconds

## All Endings

Zombie Quest has **multiple endings** based on your choices with Maya.

### Ending Conditions

Your ending is determined by:
1. Your chosen backstory (Purist/Sellout/Survivor)
2. Dialogue choices with Maya
3. Flags set during the game

### Ending Summaries

<details>
<summary><strong>Ending 1: Redemption (Best Ending)</strong></summary>

**How to Achieve**:
- During Maya dialogue, choose compassionate options
- Accept Maya fully, infected or not
- Set flag: `maya_saved`, `maya_forgiven`, `redemption_achieved`

**Outcome**: You and Maya reconcile. She fights the infection and you perform one last show together. Hope for the future.

**Emotional Tone**: Cathartic, hopeful, redemptive
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Ending 2: Bittersweet (Good Ending)</strong></summary>

**How to Achieve**:
- Choose to have one last show together
- Accept that Maya may not be fully human by the end
- Set flag: `maya_saved`, `bittersweet_ending`, `stayed_for_show`

**Outcome**: You perform an legendary final show. Maya's transformation continues but the music transcends humanity.

**Emotional Tone**: Melancholic but beautiful
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Ending 3: Transcendence (Strange Ending)</strong></summary>

**How to Achieve**:
- Accept Maya's offer to transform together
- Choose "Transform me too"
- Set flag: `maya_transformed`, `player_transformed`, `transcendence_ending`

**Outcome**: You both become something more than human. No longer bound by mortality, you create music for eternity.

**Emotional Tone**: Strange, transcendent, otherworldly
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Ending 4: Abandonment (Bad Ending)</strong></summary>

**How to Achieve**:
- Reject Maya when she needs you most
- Choose selfish or cruel dialogue options
- Set flag: `maya_abandoned`, `guilt_path` or `cruel_choice`

**Outcome**: Maya transforms alone. You escape but live with the guilt of abandoning her.

**Emotional Tone**: Hollow, regretful, tragic
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Ending 5: Hollow Victory (Worst Ending)</strong></summary>

**How to Achieve**:
- Choose the most selfish options
- Refuse to help Maya
- Set flag: `chose_selfishness`, `hollow_victory_ending`

**Outcome**: You "win" - you escape, you survive, you perform. But you've lost everything that mattered. Maya is gone.

**Emotional Tone**: Pyrrhic, empty, regretful
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Ending 6: Maya Lost (Neutral Ending)</strong></summary>

**How to Achieve**:
- Refuse transformation but part on good terms
- Set flag: `maya_lost`, `rejected_transformation`, `maya_transformed_alone`

**Outcome**: Maya chooses to become something new. You remain human. You go your separate ways, respecting each other's choices.

**Emotional Tone**: Melancholic, respectful, bittersweet
</details>

### Ending Checklist

To see all endings:
1. Save before Maya confrontation
2. Make different choices
3. Reload and try again

**Total Endings**: 6 distinct endings based on your choices

## Tips for New Players

### General Tips

1. **LOOK at everything first** - Information is key
2. **Save often** - Use the pause menu to save
3. **Talk to all NPCs** - They give crucial items and info
4. **Musical items are reusable** - Don't be afraid to use them
5. **Zombies have patterns** - Learn to predict their movement

### Combat Tips

1. **Don't fight zombies** - You can't kill them, only pacify
2. **Use walkable zones** - Stay in safe areas
3. **Musical items create zones** - Stand in safe spots and use items
4. **Invincibility frames** - Use the 1.5s after damage to escape
5. **Health is precious** - You only have 3 hearts

### Puzzle Tips

1. **Examine everything** - Clues are in descriptions
2. **Talk to everyone** - NPCs often hint at solutions
3. **Items have specific uses** - If an item doesn't work, try another
4. **Dialogue choices matter** - They affect endings
5. **No dead ends** - You can't get permanently stuck

### Exploration Tips

1. **Map the area mentally** - Remember room connections
2. **Return to old areas** - New items may appear
3. **Check all hotspots** - Some are easy to miss
4. **Flavor text is fun** - Read everything for atmosphere
5. **Multiple visits reveal more** - Repeated examinations give new text

## Secrets and Easter Eggs

### Hidden Details

1. **Memory System**: Examine the same hotspot 3+ times to trigger protagonist memories
2. **Room-Specific Music**: Each room has unique procedural music layers
3. **Zombie Names**: Some zombies are named after real Minneapolis musicians
4. **Historical References**: First Avenue starred in Prince's "Purple Rain"
5. **Let It Be Records**: Real Minneapolis record store from the 1980s

### Optional Content

- **Bathroom Easter Egg**: Maya's graffiti tag appears in multiple locations
- **Vinyl Vault**: Hidden rare records with band backstory
- **Promoter's Office**: Discover the dark truth behind the outbreak
- **Loading Dock**: Alternative escape route (doesn't affect ending)

### Achievement-Style Challenges

Try these self-imposed challenges:

- **Pacifist Run**: Never get hit by zombies
- **Speed Run**: Complete game in minimum time
- **Perfect Ending**: Get the Redemption ending
- **Completionist**: Find all items and visit all rooms
- **Dialogue Master**: Explore all dialogue branches
